All About Me

Early Life

No one (not even me!) knows exactly how old I am. I spent most of my life so far as a stray. Eventually, some people found me and took me to the Humane Society of Huron Valley. They figured out that I was about 6 years old! They kept calling me Kramer, for some reason. Despite the weird name, they cleaned me up, made sure I wasn't being bothed by fleas or other pests, fed me lots of yummy food, and gave me a cozy place to live indoors. Although all the people at HSHV were very nice, I only lived there for about a week. On October 11th, 2015, my day started out very normally, but it ended completely differently than I thought it would.
